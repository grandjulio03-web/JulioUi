Air Command

By default the nav sits hidden, just a toggle in the white bar. Hit the hamburger and the links glide in from the left (or set `data-airbar-position="right"` to mirror it) while the CTA can be any `.jf-btn` variant.

Testing tips

Update `_navigation.scss`, run `npm run build-css`, and refresh this fixture to confirm the sliding panel and aside keep their premium polish.

Badge playground

Badges live in `_forms.scss` but you can drop them anywhere. Mix `.badge` with a color variant and size, then wrap them in `.badge-chip` when you need descriptive labels.
