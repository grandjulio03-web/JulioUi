Air Tables

Same ideas as Bootstrap tables (table-striped, table-bordered, table-hover, table-sm), but one wrapper + modifiers: no dependency, all five palette tones (ace, beta, gamma, delta, epsilon), sticky header, accent rail, and dark variant.

All five framework colors

Add one tone class on .air-table: air-table--ace, air-table--beta, air-table--gamma, air-table--delta, or air-table--epsilon. Default without a tone matches ace.

Bootstrap-style vs Air

Typical pain points with Bootstrap tables alone: generic look, many chained classes, heavier bundle, and theming often fights your brand tokens. Air tables stay minimal in markup, use your palette (ace/beta/gamma/delta), add rail + sticky + dark without extra plugins.
